About WhatsOn

WhatsOn is your go-to platform for discovering live events, streaming performances, and booking venues in your area. Whether you're a fan of street performances, jazz nights, or theater, we make it easy for you to explore and enjoy live entertainment.

Our mission is to connect people with the vibrant world of live events while also supporting local artists and performers. With WhatsOn, you'll never miss out on an event again!
